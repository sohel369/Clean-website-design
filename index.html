Frequently Asked Questions

Is this stuff really free? What's the catch?

Yes, all packages have no hidden fees. All we ask in return is for you to attend a presentation at a partner Vegas resort, with no obligations to purchase anything to receive your gifts.

What do I need to do to get started?

To get started, simply select a package from our offerings and complete the quick sign-up form. No payment info is needed - just your name, email and phone number. We'll only use it for reservation purposes.

What is the guided tour? What can I expect?

The guided tour is an informative visit to one of our partner resorts. You'll learn about the resort's amenities and offerings to save money on your next hotel stay. There's no obligation to buy, sign, or join anything.

Can I choose the date and time of my tour?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling options and will help find the best day and time availability for you.

What if I need more show tickets or vouchers?

Have more guests? Our friendly concierge is here to help. For participating you'll receive exclusive access to purchase our show tickets, dinner vouchers, and more at our wholesale prices.

What if I have more questions?

Our local team of Vacation Buddies are based here in Las Vegas and are available to assist you by phone seven days per week, from 9am-6pm PST.
